ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program

Empowered by Faith, Sustained by Generosity

Welcome to a divine initiative grounded in the promises of Scripture and the power of community. ABBA's Kingdom Donation Program is more than a financial tool—it is a Kingdom movement operating under the protective framework of a Private Membership Association (PMA). Through this sacred structure, your membership ensures every seed sown is stewarded in alignment with God’s Word and Kingdom principles.

Your one-time donation of $1,222 is an act of obedience and faith, sowing into fertile ground for both spiritual and tangible harvests. Under the PMA, 90% of contributions are returned to members through affiliate earnings, creating opportunities for financial empowerment and Kingdom growth. The remaining 10% is divided to advance the Kingdom Vision (9%) and honor the mission's co-creator (1%), ensuring a faithful alignment with the principles of sowing and reaping.

As a member of this PMA, you participate in a covenant to build ABBA’s Kingdom by:

  • Restoring Families: Supporting faith-based centers for mentorship, counseling, and financial education.
  • Eradicating Poverty: Funding sustainable projects that empower underserved communities.
  • Advancing the Gospel: Resourcing ministries, outreach programs, and church-building initiatives worldwide.

Join this sacred mission today, knowing that your seed will yield a bountiful harvest—not just for yourself, but for the countless lives impacted by ABBA's provision.
